PLAINT

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHEWETH:

1. That the plaintiff is a partnership firm engaged in the business of trading in electronic goods and is duly registered under the Indian Partnership Act, 1932.

2. That the defendant is a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, engaged in the business of distribution of electronic goods.

3. That on 1st January 2024, the plaintiff and the defendant entered into a written agreement for the supply of 1000 LED televisions of 55-inch size at the rate of Rs. 25,000 per unit, totaling Rs. 2,50,00,000 (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakhs only).

4. That as per the terms of the agreement, the defendant was required to deliver the goods within 30 days from the date of the agreement, i.e., by 31st January 2024.

5. That the plaintiff had made an advance payment of Rs. 50,00,000 (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) to the defendant on 5th January 2024.

6. That despite repeated requests and reminders, the defendant has failed to deliver the goods within the stipulated time and has also refused to refund the advance payment.

7. That the defendant's failure to perform its contractual obligations has caused substantial loss and damage to the plaintiff's business.

CAUSE OF ACTION:

The cause of action for this suit arose on 31st January 2024, when the defendant failed to deliver the goods within the stipulated time, and continues to arise on each day when the defendant refuses to perform its contractual obligations or refund the advance payment.

JURISDICTION:

This Hon'ble Court has jurisdiction to try and determine this suit as:
a) The defendant carries on business within the jurisdiction of this Court;
b) The cause of action has arisen within the jurisdiction of this Court;
c) The agreement was executed within the jurisdiction of this Court.

VALUATION:

The suit is valued at Rs. 2,50,00,000 (Rupees Two Crore Fifty Lakhs only) for the purpose of court fees and jurisdiction.

RELIEFS CLAIMED:

The plaintiff, therefore, prays for:

a) A decree for specific performance of the agreement dated 1st January 2024, directing the defendant to deliver 1000 LED televisions of 55-inch size to the plaintiff;

b) In the alternative, a decree for recovery of Rs. 50,00,000 (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only) being the advance payment made by the plaintiff to the defendant;

c) A decree for Rs. 10,00,000 (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) as damages for breach of contract;

d) A decree for Rs. 5,00,000 (Rupees Five Lakhs only) as interest at the rate of 12% per annum from 1st February 2024 till the date of payment;

e) A decree for costs of the suit;

f) Any other relief that this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts and circumstances of the case.
