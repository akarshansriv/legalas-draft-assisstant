PETITIONER MOST RESPECTFULLY SHEWETH:

1. That the petitioner is a law-abiding citizen of India and a resident of Dwarka, New Delhi.

2. That on 15th March 2024, the petitioner was illegally detained by the police officials of Police Station Dwarka without any valid reason or arrest warrant.

3. That the petitioner's fundamental rights guaranteed under Articles 14, 19, and 21 of the Constitution of India have been grossly violated by the respondents.

4. That the petitioner was subjected to custodial torture and inhuman treatment during the period of illegal detention.

5. That the petitioner has made several representations to the authorities but no action has been taken so far.

GROUNDS:

A. VIOLATION OF ARTICLE 14:
   The petitioner has been discriminated against and treated differently from other citizens without any reasonable classification, which violates the right to equality.

B. VIOLATION OF ARTICLE 19:
   The illegal detention has deprived the petitioner of his fundamental right to move freely throughout the territory of India.

C. VIOLATION OF ARTICLE 21:
   The petitioner's right to life and personal liberty has been violated through illegal detention and custodial torture.

PRAYER:

In view of the above facts and circumstances, it is most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to:

a) Issue a writ of habeas corpus directing the respondents to produce the petitioner before this Hon'ble Court if still in custody;

b) Issue a writ of mandamus directing the respondents to:
   - Conduct a thorough investigation into the illegal detention and custodial torture;
   - Take appropriate disciplinary action against the erring police officials;
   - Compensate the petitioner for the violation of his fundamental rights;

c) Issue a writ of prohibition restraining the respondents from further harassing or detaining the petitioner without due process of law;

d) Pass such other order or orders as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts and circumstances of the case.

VERIFICATION:

I, RAMESH KUMAR SHARMA, the petitioner herein, do hereby solemnly affirm and state that the contents of paragraphs 1 to 5 are true to my knowledge and the contents of paragraphs A to C are true to my information and belief, and no part of it is false and nothing material has been concealed therefrom.
